Special Annuities

Ordinary annuity vs. annuity due

14

Ordinary annuity

Cash flows occur at the end of each period. Excel type = 0 or blank.

Loans | savings deposits

Annuity due

Cash flows occur at the beginning of each period. Excel type = 1.

Rent | leases

Annuity due values are ordinary annuity values multiplied by (1 + r), because each payment gets one extra period.

Perpetuity

The forever cash flow

15

Definition

A perpetuity is an annuity with equal cash flows that continue forever. Preferred stock is a common example.

PV = PMT / r
Strategy Summary

The same TVM engine works for both sides.

Investor

Higher frequency helps you. Time is your greatest asset. Goal: maximize compounding.

Borrower

Higher frequency hurts you. Longer terms lower payments, but raise total cost. Goal: minimize interest.

Credit Cards

APR tells you the quote. EAR tells you the cost.

17
APR = periodic rate x periods per year
EAR = (1 + APR/m)^m - 1
When compounding frequency changes, compare EAR before choosing the lower-looking APR.
